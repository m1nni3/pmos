🎯 The real difference:

Option A tells you Oakdale was R 1,929 "better" in March. Good to know — but was it because rental came in higher, or because maintenance was R 685 under budget, or because no levy was paid (that's actually a problem)? You can't tell.

Option B shows the R 1,929 is a mix: maintenance under (good), rental over (good), but levy collected R 0 (bad — tenant hasn't paid). You spot the red flag immediately.

TL;DR: If you just want green/red → Option A (ships today). If you want to know what drove the green/red → Option B (needs a D1 table + seed data).
